6-Month Summary: From Route 6 Repairman to the “Lemon Project” Originator(The 40th Milestone)

30/03/2026

Route6_Rider

 

 

H1：6 Months of Bytes and Soil

In late September 2025, I purchased CamTravel.xyz and began recording under challenging hardware and network conditions. Six months later, I’ve evolved from an observer to a field experimenter. My workbench (Image 1) displays this evolution: from a soldering iron for appliance repairs, to bank passbooks auditing 8 years of history, to pruning shears for fruit tree grafting.

 

H2: Video Genesis

My video documentation began in August 2025. The YouTube channel CamTravel Field Reports captured early pet videos. Despite having only 3 subscribers then, those 1,000+ views proved the inherent appeal of authentic life.

 

H3: The Lemon Project & Community

I plan to graft lemon scions onto two struggling citrus rootstocks. This opportunity stems from community goodwill built through voluntary electronics repairs. Despite the challenge of solo recording, this fieldwork remains our core mission.
