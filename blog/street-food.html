The $1.5 Lifestyle

17/03/2026

Route6_Rider

On a dusty street corner in Phnom Penh, modern coffee culture is quietly blooming. A sleek K-CAF booth stands in stark contrast to its gritty surroundings. For about $1.5 (6000 Riels), you get more than just caffeine; you get a glimpse into the changing tastes of Cambodia’s younger generation. It’s where tradition meets a new daily rhythm.
