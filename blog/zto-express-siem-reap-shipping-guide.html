Moving to Siem Reap often means realizing that local international shipping can be incredibly expensive. While DHL and FedEx are reliable, their rates can be a shock to your budget. Enter ZTO Express (中通快递)—a game-changer for expats who want to ship goods from China (via Taobao or 1688) at a fraction of the cost.

1. Why Choose ZTO Over DHL?

The primary reason is cost. ZTO specializes in the China-Cambodia route. If you are ordering household items, electronics, or outdoor gear from China, ZTO offers a “consolidated shipping” service. You ship your items to their warehouse in China, and they bring them to Siem Reap for a much lower weight-based fee.

2. How It Works: The “Taobao + ABA” Strategy

Many locals and savvy expats use their ABA Bank app to pay directly on Taobao.

  1. Set up Taobao: Use a translation tool to navigate.
  2. Shipping Address: Set your shipping address to ZTO’s China transit warehouse (ask the Siem Reap office for their latest warehouse code).
  3. Tracking: Once it arrives in Cambodia, you’ll get a notification via phone or the app.

 

3. Picking Up Your Parcel in Siem Reap

The ZTO collection point in Siem Reap is a hub of activity. You’ll often see a mix of locals and expats picking up everything from small parcels to large furniture.

  • Location: Most ZTO points are centrally located near the old market or main roads (National Highway 6).
  • What to bring: Just your phone with the tracking number or the SMS notification.
